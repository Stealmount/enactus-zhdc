About Us

Enactus ZHDC is the official entrepreneurship society of Zakir Husain Delhi College, University of Delhi. We are part of Enactus India — the world's largest experiential learning platform connecting student, academic and business leaders through entrepreneurial action.

Since our founding, we have built ventures that touch real lives — from artisan communities in Old Delhi to farming families in rural Haryana. We compete at regional and national Enactus competitions, representing our college on India's biggest stages.
